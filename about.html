Driven by a passion to create and a love for capturing images, Connor Farrell brings his photographic expertise and design knowledge to every project.
Connor Farrell is based out of Ottawa, he studied at Mohawk College in Hamilton where he furthered his Photographic talents and his knowledge. In his first semester, Connor was published as an up and coming photographer in PhotoEd’s Fall 2017 Magazine. Later, he would win first place in the On Location category in Mohawk’s Photo Night. Since graduation, Connor has worked with local business and international companies such as Black Cellar Wine, and Glassy Eyewear, to create images for publication and social media purposes.

Connor recently graduated from Algonquin College's Interactive Media Design program in 2022. During his education at Algonquin, Connor learnt how to create and design websites using modern coding languages such as HTML,CSS, and JAVA . Connor also expanded his artistic skills to be able to create digital graphics and logos by using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
